Our Services

Our Healthcare Departments

Our healthcare departments offer comprehensive services including emergency medicine, primary care, specialty clinics, surgical services, maternity and pediatrics, diagnostic imaging, rehabilitation, and mental health support. Each department is staffed by skilled professionals dedicated to providing high-quality care tailored to individual needs.

Cardiology

Nandi Hospital's cardiologists specialize in heart care, offering personalized treatment plans for each patient. With a focus on evidence-based medicine and advanced interventions, we strive to optimize heart health and improve overall well-being.

Dermatology

Nandi Hospital's dermatologists offer personalized care for skin, hair, and nail concerns. Through advanced treatments and expertise, we prioritize patient well-being, providing tailored solutions for healthy skin and enhanced confidence.

ENT

Nandi Hospital's specialists offer personalized care for ear, nose, and throat concerns, employing advanced techniques for optimal outcomes. With a focus on patient comfort, we strive to improve well-being through tailored treatment plans and effective interventions.

Gastroenterology

Nandi Hospital's gastrologists specialize in digestive health. With expertise and compassion, we prioritize patient well-being, offering tailored treatments for improved gastrointestinal health and enhanced quality of life outcomes.

General Physician

Nandi Hospital's general physician specialists deliver personalized care for adult patients. From preventive screenings to managing chronic illnesses, we emphasize evidence-based medicine to promote overall health and longevity.

General Surgeon

Nandi Hospital's surgeons provide personalized surgical care. With expertise and compassion, we prioritize patient well-being, offering tailored treatments for improved surgical outcomes and enhanced quality of life enhancement.

Gynecology

Nandi Hospital's gynecologists provide personalized care for women's health needs. From routine screenings to specialized treatments, we prioritize evidence-based medicine to support every woman's well-being and reproductive health.

Orthopedics

Nandi Hospital's expert orthopedic team offers advanced care for musculoskeletal conditions. From fractures to joint replacements, we prioritize minimally invasive procedures and evidence-based treatments to restore mobility and improve patient well-being.

Pediatrics

At Nandi Hospital, our dedicated pediatric team delivers compassionate care tailored to every child's needs. From preventive check-ups to managing illnesses, we prioritize evidence-based approaches to ensure each child thrives.

Psychiatry

Nandi Hospital's psychiatrists provide personalized mental health care. With compassion and expertise, we prioritize patient well-being, offering tailored treatment plans for improved mental wellness and quality of life enhancement.

Plastic-surgery

Nandi Hospital's plastic surgeons provide personalized aesthetic and reconstructive treatments. Utilizing advanced techniques, we prioritize patient well-being, delivering natural-looking results and enhancing confidence with tailored care plans.

Pulmonology

Nandi Hospital's pulmonologists focus on respiratory health, delivering tailored care for lung conditions. Through evidence-based approaches, we tirelessly strive to improve lung function and promote overall well-being for every patient.

Physiotherapy

Nandi Hospital's dedicated physiotherapists provide personalized rehabilitation plans for patients. Utilizing a range of techniques, we aim to restore mobility and enhance quality of life, prioritizing individualized care for optimal recovery.

Radiology

Nandi Hospital's radiologists specialize in tailored diagnostic imaging. Utilizing advanced technology and expertise, we provide accurate interpretations for effective treatment planning and management strategies tailored to individual needs.

Urology

Nandi Hospital's urologists specialize in treating urinary tract and reproductive system conditions with advanced medical techniques. Our personalized approach ensures optimal urological health and improved well-being for every patient.

Neurology

Nandi Hospital's neurologists specialize in brain and nervous system disorders, offering personalized care. With evidence-based medicine and advanced treatments, we aim to optimize neurological health and enhance quality of life.

Neurosurgery

At Nandi Hospital, our neurosurgeons provide specialized care for brain and nervous system conditions. With a focus on evidence-based medicine and advanced surgical interventions, we strive to enhance neurological health and patient well-being.
